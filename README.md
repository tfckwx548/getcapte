## What Actually Is Capte?

Capte is a web-based application that uses AI to automatically transcribe, subtitle, and transform your videos into engaging content. Founded in 2020 by Emmanuel Geoffray and Gabriel Delattre (who were frustrated with how time-consuming and expensive subtitling was), it's grown to serve over 60,000 users.

The basic workflow is ridiculously straightforward. You upload your video, their AI transcribes the audio in seconds, you customize the style to match your brand, and you're done. You can export the subtitled video, create clips from it, or even translate it into multiple languages.

## The Features That Actually Matter

### Automatic Transcription
Upload a video and Capte's AI analyzes the speech and generates subtitles within a minute. The accuracy is solid, though you'll want to do a quick review for technical terms or names.

### Video Clips Generation
Turn one long video into multiple short clips perfect for social media. The platform can automatically identify key moments and create shareable snippets â€” massive time-saver for content repurposing.

### Translation
Need your content in Spanish, German, French, or ten other languages? Capte handles automatic translation. Just transcribe in the original language, select your target language, and you're set.

### Style Customization
Hundreds of pre-made themes let you match your brand colors, fonts, and visual identity. You can also create custom styles and save them for future use.

### Magic Emojis
The AI can automatically insert relevant emojis into your subtitles. Sounds gimmicky, but it actually works well for casual social content.

### Safe Zones
Built-in guides for different social platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) help you position your subtitles where they won't get cut off by UI elements.

### Export Options
Get your video with embedded subtitles in Full HD or 4K quality, or export just the subtitle file (.srt or .vtt) to use elsewhere.
